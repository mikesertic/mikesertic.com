Advocates for Self-Government

President

The Advocates for Self-Government is a national nonprofit organization dedicated to political education and the principles of self-governance. Its flagship creation, the World's Smallest Political Quiz, has been taken by over 30 million people since its launch more than 40 years ago, making it one of the most widely used political assessment tools in history.

As President, Mike oversees organizational strategy, fundraising, and program development — including initiatives like Quiz in the Classroom, which brings nonpartisan political education into schools; Operation Politically Homeless, an outreach program for public events; and the Liberty Bounty program, which incentivizes grassroots engagement.

Political DNA

Creator

Political DNA is an independent political assessment platform that moves beyond the traditional left-right spectrum. Rather than placing users on a single axis, it maps them across 32 distinct political archetypes — providing a far more nuanced, accurate, and useful picture of where someone actually stands on the issues that matter.

The platform is designed to be shareable, engaging, and genuinely informative — built as a modern alternative to oversimplified political quizzes that flatten complex beliefs into binary categories.

Self-Management Institute

Morning Star Company — Previously

Morning Star Company is one of the world's largest tomato processing companies and operates without traditional management hierarchy — a radical organizational model known as self-management. At the Self-Management Institute, Mike studied and helped advance these principles: how large-scale industrial organizations can operate through voluntary agreements between colleagues rather than top-down authority.

This work shaped his thinking about how governance, coordination, and individual agency intersect at every scale — from companies to communities to countries.

Students For Liberty

Founding Team

Students For Liberty is one of the largest international networks of pro-liberty student organizations in the world, with chapters on campuses across dozens of countries. Mike was part of the founding team that built the organization from its earliest days, helping create the infrastructure and programming that empowered young people to organize around principles of individual freedom, free markets, and limited government.
