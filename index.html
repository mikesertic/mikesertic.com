Mike Sertic is President of the Advocates for Self-Government, the organization behind the World's Smallest Political Quiz — the most widely taken political quiz in history, with over 30 million participants across four decades.

He is also the creator of Political DNA, a next-generation assessment that maps people across 32 political archetypes — because the real political landscape is far more interesting than left versus right.
