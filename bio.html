about mike sertic

Mike Sertic serves as President of the Advocates for Self-Government, a national nonprofit dedicated to political education and promoting the principles of self-governance. Under his leadership, the organization continues to steward the World's Smallest Political Quiz — a tool that has reached over 30 million people across its 40-year history — alongside educational programs like Quiz in the Classroom and Operation Politically Homeless.

He is also the creator of Political DNA, an independent political assessment platform that moves beyond the traditional left-right spectrum to categorize individuals into 32 distinct political archetypes — giving people a far more nuanced understanding of their own political identity.

Previously, Mike worked at the Self-Management Institute at Morning Star Company, where he studied and helped advance non-hierarchical organizational structures operating at industrial scale. He was also a founding team member of Students For Liberty, one of the largest international networks of pro-liberty student organizations in the world.

His career has consistently focused on translating complex ideas about governance, liberty, and human organization into practical tools and systems that empower individuals to think — and govern — for themselves.
